## Phase 2: Create Meta Business Account (10 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Meta Business Suite
1. Visit https://business.facebook.com/
2. Click **"Create Account"** (top right)
3. Fill in:
   - Business Name: `Your Business Name`
   - Your Name: `Your Full Name`
   - Business Email: `your-email@domain.com`
4. Click **"Next"**

### Step 2: Add Business Details
1. Enter business information:
   - Legal business name
   - Business address
   - Phone number
   - Website URL
2. Select your country
3. Click **"Submit"**

### Step 3: Verify Your Business
1. Meta will send verification code to your email
2. Enter the code
3. Your Meta Business Account is now created! ‚úÖ

---

## Phase 3: Apply for WhatsApp Business API (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Access WhatsApp in Business Manager
1. In Meta Business Suite, click **"All Tools"** (left sidebar)
2. Find and click **"WhatsApp"**
3. Click **"Get Started"** button

### Step 2: Choose Your Setup Option

You'll see three options:

**Option A: Cloud API (Recommended)**
- ‚úÖ Hosted by Meta
- ‚úÖ Easiest to manage
- ‚úÖ No server setup needed
- **Choose this one!**

**Option B: On-Premises API**
- ‚ùå You host it yourself
- ‚ùå More complex
- Skip this

**Option C: Business Solution Provider**
- Partner handles everything
- Good option too (360Dialog, MessageBird)

### Step 3: Select Cloud API
1. Click **"Cloud API"**
2. Click **"Get Started"**

### Step 4: Create a WhatsApp Business Account
1. Fill in business details:
   - Display name: `Your Business Name`
   - Category: Select your business type (e.g., "Restaurant", "Retail Shop")
   - Description: Brief description of your business
   - Business address
   - Business email
   - Business website
2. Upload your business logo (optional but recommended)
3. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 5: Accept Terms & Conditions
1. Read WhatsApp Business Terms
2. Check the box to accept
3. Click **"Continue"**

### Step 6: Add Phone Number

**IMPORTANT:** You can't use a number that's already on WhatsApp!

**Option A: Add New Number**
1. Enter a phone number that's NOT registered on WhatsApp
2. You'll receive SMS verification code
3. Enter the code
4. This becomes your first WhatsApp Business number ‚úÖ

**Option B: Buy Number from Partner** (Easier)
1. Click "I don't have a phone number"
2. Meta will show partner options (360Dialog, Vonage)
3. Sign up with partner
4. They'll provide you with numbers

### Step 7: Review and Submit
1. Review all information
2. Click **"Submit for Review"**
3. Meta will review your application (1-2 weeks)
